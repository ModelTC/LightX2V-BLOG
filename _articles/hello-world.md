Welcome to my blog. This is the first post, written to verify everything is working.

## Why blog?

A few reasons:

1. **Clarify thinking** — Writing forces vague ideas into precise sentences
2. **Leave a record** — Reading old posts years later shows how much you've changed
3. **Connect with people** — Someone out there might feel exactly the same way

> Writing is an extension of thinking, not merely a record of it.

## What comes next

Not sure yet. Probably a mix of technical notes, book thoughts, and observations from daily life. The goal is to write consistently and let the focus emerge naturally.
